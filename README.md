# Grocery List App

## What Are We Solving? 
Many people rely on paper grocery lists or basic notes apps, which are easy to lose, hard to share, and do not update well when plans change. Our app will solve this by giving users a simple, focused grocery list on their phone where items are easy to add, organize, and check off while shopping. Because most people already carry their phone in the store, a mobile app is a practical way to fix this problem.
